Đáp án/transcript: Part 2 Thoát

Sample TOEIC Test 15

  • Part 2

Hiện Transcript

How many days will Greg be away?

(A) For a vacation.

(B) Three in total.

(C) I’ll tell her.

7
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Where did you buy this pen?

(A) That’ll be two dollars.

(B) I liked the color.

(C) At the bookstore.

8
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

Could you help me put labels on these boxes?

(A) I’d be happy to help.

(B) Many different sizes.

(C) Some old computer parts.

9
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Are you going to call the caterer about the change?

(A) I don’t have her number.

(B) Kate’s change of clothes.

(C) We ate at a restaurant.

10
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

You’ve used this software before, haven’t you?

(A) Please fill in this spreadsheet.

(B) In the user manual.

(C) Yes, but that was years ago.

11
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

Why did you arrive late to work today?

(A) Sure, if that’s what you need.

(B) Because I missed the bus.

(C) Before the afternoon shift.

12
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Didn’t Ga-Yoon make an agenda for the meeting?

(A) The whole department.

(B) Not that I know of.

(C) Where is Conference Room Four?

13
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

The park’s operating hours have been reduced

because of budget cuts.

(A) I’m sorry to hear that.

(B) The staff parking area.

(C) He’s on the operations team.

14
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

When will you take your lunch break?

(A) The café next door.

(B) Take as many as you want.

(C) As soon as I finish reading this report.

15
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

What award was your book nominated for?

(A) By an authors’ organization.

(B) Best Historical Fiction.

(C) You really deserve it.

16
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

How can I tell if the batteries need to be replaced?

(A) Two should be enough for it.

(B) This light will start flashing.

(C) Oh, that’s my favorite place.

17
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Should I reschedule the building permit inspection, or not?

(A) It used to be an office building.

(B) The city planning department.

(C) Yes—we need more time to prepare.

18
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

This medicine is sold at most pharmacies, right?

(A) I’ve definitely seen it on display at Cardell’s.

(B) The patient in Room 405.

(C) No, it’s a medical seminar.

19
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

How about sending an electronic invoice for the work?

(A) This client prefers a paper one.

(B) Sorry, I already spent it.

(C) Once a month.

20
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

How do I access my bank account online?

(A) You can pay bills more easily.

(B) Yes, both savings and checking.

(C) Have you registered a password yet?

21
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

Who’s designing the invitations for the club banquet?

(A) That’s what the organizer said.

(B) One of our members is a graphic designer.

(C) I appreciate your inviting me.

22
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Is this the updated list of exhibitors at the trade show?

(A) It looks like the old one.

(B) Kojima Tools’ demonstration.

(C) No, the first show is on April eighth.

23
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Which photograph are you going to use with this

article?

(A) Thanks, it took me a while to write.

(B) Look straight into the camera, please.

(C) There aren’t a lot of options.

24
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

Where should I order the banner for the anniversary party?

(A) Talk to Sarah about that.

(B) How about the back wall?

(C) A simple message of congratulations.

25
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

I figured out why the packaging machine broke down.

(A) It’s just down the street.

(B) No, the package wasn’t delivered.

(C) Will it take long to fix it?

26
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

Why don’t we make a corporate donation to a local charity?

(A) We’re a major corporation.

(B) I like that idea.

(C) It was extremely helpful.

27
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Didn’t you get the survey I e-mailed to you?

(A) It wasn’t in my inbox.

(B) There’s no mail on Sundays.

(C) I decided not to.

28
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Do you want to change the flight’s departure date or return date?

(A) Actually, I’m canceling the trip.

(B) He departed from Rome.

(C) First-class section.

29
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Have you reviewed the latest version of the grant proposal?

(A) It wasn’t late—the deadline’s today.

(B) A research project on alternative energy.

(C) I think it’s ready to be submitted.

30
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

Why are we postponing the ceremony to next week?

(A) Until the end of the month.

(B) You’ve seen the weather forecast.

(C) A post with the meeting agenda details.

31
Đáp án đúng:B
