Sample TOEIC Test 18

  • Part 2

Hiện Transcript

When are you coming in to the office tomorrow?

(A) Not until nine-fifteen.

(B) To organize my files.

(C) The marketing department.

7
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Did you get my e-mail about the conference?

(A) The post office was closed.

(B) No, I’ve been away.

(C) All of the presenters.

8
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Where should we send the car to pick up the clients?

(A) By express mail.

(B) I’ll pick it up this morning.

(C) To the Greel Plaza Hotel.

9
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

Can’t you take an earlier train on Friday afternoon?

(A) Because it’s scheduled for Friday.

(B) All staff have attended the training.

(C) I’ll need to check when I can leave work.

10
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

Will Ms. Dillinger give us more details about the company trip soon?

(A) Well, it’s still six months away.

(B) Try your confirmation code

(C) It was quite nice of her.

11
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Do you think you can repair the copy machine, or will we need to buy a new one?

(A) About an hour.

(B) Yes, it was very affordable.

(C) I’m certain it can be fixed.

12
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

Who posted the notice in the break room?

(A) Sure, go ahead and take a break.

(B) Someone from Personnel.

(C) Above the water cooler.

13
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

How do you report a problem with this software?

(A) What kind of problem are you having?

(B) Since the last update.

(C) She’s a newspaper reporter.

14
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Where will this year’s summer charity concert be held?

(A) Usually in the middle of July.

(B) We plan to offer free music classes.

(C) I heard it’ll be in Baxter Park.

15
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

Which airline did you use when you flew to the Bahamas?

(A) I always stay on the beach.

(B) Just one large suitcase.

(C) It was SlimJet Air.

16
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

It’s not supposed to rain this evening, is it?

(A) I haven’t checked the forecast.

(B) They’re running late.

(C) Oh, was I supposed to?

17
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Who’s going to enter these expenses into the database?

(A) None of us has access to it.

(B) Wow, that is expensive.

(C) Through the building’s side entrance.

18
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Why was our plant in Taiwan closed down?

(A) No, I don’t think so.

(B) The one near the harbor.

(C) It didn’t pass an important inspection.

19
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

How often should we clean out the staff refrigerator?

(A) At least once a month.

(B) The chicken sandwich is mine.

(C) Cleaning supplies are in the storage closet.

20
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Has all of the ink for the printer been used up already?

(A) It hasn’t been decided yet.

(B) Yes, but we’ve ordered more.

(C) Handouts for the seminar.

21
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Ms. Shin has been transferred to the Berlin branch, hasn’t she?

(A) The responsibilities of her new position.

(B) She started working there yesterday.

(C) How much did she send?

22
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

I’ll let you know when your table is ready.

(A) Thanks—we’ll wait here.

(B) I’ve been to that restaurant before.

(C) A darker brown wood.

23
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Why don’t you give this assignment to our intern?

(A) I’ll put up a few more signs.

(B) He already has enough to do.

(C) Researching our competitors.

24
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Do you want to buy my tickets for the film premiere this weekend?

(A) It’s the best film I’ve seen in a while.

(B) The “Tickets” page of the Web site.

(C) I’m not sure I’ll be able to go.

25
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

This uniform design is the most popular with employees.

(A) Let’s hope customers like it, too.

(B) Which brand does she design for?

(C) The red shirt and black pants.

26
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Should I begin the workshop by introducing myself or the agenda?

(A) OK—my name’s Camille and I’m an engineer.

(B) A quick game might relax the participants.

(C) The session on leadership begins at three P.M.

27
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

This set of keys belongs to you, doesn’t it?

(A) No, not that long.

(B) Yes, where did you find them?

(C) There’s a spare set at the reception desk.

28
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Why did the delivery person come up the stairs?

(A) He’ll arrive just before lunch.

(B) Please move them somewhere else.

(C) The elevator must be out of order.

29
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

How about meeting at the coffee shop to discuss the project?

(A) She said we’ve sold ten cups so far.

(B) We talked about several topics.

(C) I’d rather use the meeting room.

30
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

Have we found enough volunteers for the focus group?

(A) They’ll test our newest products.

(B) I believe Chris is still searching.

(C) That should be plenty of space.

31
Đáp án đúng:B
