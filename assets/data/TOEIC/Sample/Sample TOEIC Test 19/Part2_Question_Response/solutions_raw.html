(B) Let’s get together again soon.

(C) Everyone except Bill.

(B) About five hours.

(C) It’s a great place to visit.

Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

(B) No, I didn’t know he was hiring.

(C) Yes, I heard about it this morning.

Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

Đáp án đúng:A

Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

(A) The office supply store is closed today.

(B) Look in the desk drawer.

(C) Do you have enough money?

Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

(C) I got home at eight o’clock.

Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

(B) Thanks, I’m really thirsty.

(C) On the conference table.

16
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

(C) Take the elevator to the second floor.

Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

(C) Dr. Nelson got an emergency call.

18
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

(A) Yes, I just made a fresh pot.

(B) Lunges are good for your body.

(C) Yes, I have an extra copy.

19
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

(A) She lives in Orlando.

(B) She’s a bank executive.

(C) She will be moving soon.

20
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

(A) I hope so.

(B) The hotel stay cost a lot of money.

(C) The air ticket was sold yesterday.

21
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Do you want to start working on the new project now or later?

(A) Actually, I started it this morning.

(B) I have to work late, too.

(C) I’m glad to hear the good news.

22
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Where should I put this box of file folders?

(A) The computer files are easy to find.

(B) Will it fit on the bottom shelf?

(C) They sell them at the local stationery store.

23
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Should we review the contract once more before signing it?

(A) Yes, I saw the sign above the graphs.

(B) No, I couldn’t reach him.

(C) Yes, I think that would be a wise thing to do.

24
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

When is your office manager planning to relocate?

(A) We need more space.

(B) As soon as she finds the right place.

(C) She manages the Human Resources Department.

25
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

I have to call my landlord about the heating system.

(A) My brother’s rent has been increased.

(B) Are you still having problems?

(C) The weather will be rainy this time of year.

26
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Would you like to reserve a compact car or a mid-sized?

(A) Whichever is cheaper.

(B) I parked in the garage.

(C) Yes, that would be fine.

27
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Why don’t you invite your friend Marcus to our office party?

(A) No, he doesn’t.

(B) Sure. I will turn it off.

(C) That’s a great idea.

28
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

Our new business cards have finally been delivered.

(A) Yes, I got mine yesterday.

(B) The cars are efficient for work.

(C) Twice a week.

29
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Don’t you need to set up the overhead projector for your presentation?

(A) A few people were present.

(B) No, I’m wearing a suit.

(C) Thanks, I almost forgot about that.

Đáp án đúng:C
