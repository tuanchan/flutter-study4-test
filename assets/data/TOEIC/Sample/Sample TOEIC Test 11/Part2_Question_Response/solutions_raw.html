Sample TOEIC Test 11

  • Part 2

Hiện Transcript

What time is the electrician scheduled to arrive?

(A) Call me anytime.

(B) Between 9 a.m. and 1 P.M.

(C) To install new lighting.

7
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Where is the nearest bus stop?

(A) Bus number 99.

(B) I drove by the coffee shop.

(C) I have no idea.

8
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

Who is in charge of catering?

(A) That would be Maria.

(B) How much did they charge?

(C) Katie is charging her phone.

9
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Would you rather sit by the window or in the aisle seat?

(A) I’ll close it if you’re cold.

(B) We don’t need another chair.

(C) It doesn’t matter.

10
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

When did you receive the achievement award?

(A) Next month’s award show.

(B) Last week at the benefit.

(C) I don’t have my receipt.

11
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Why don’t we ask Donna to guide the museum tours?

(A) Do you think she would be a good choice?

(B) They’ve been to that museum.

(C) No, I’m a tourist here myself.

12
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Who is working the late shift tonight?

(A) The technician fixed the shift key.

(B) You have a good night, too.

(C) I think Walter is.

13
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

Are you going to pick the client up at the airport?

(A) No, my assistant will do it.

(B) He already picked up his air ticket.

(C) Sony, it’s too heavy.

14
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Which hat looks better on me?

(A) It’s a lovely color.

(B) They both suit you.

(C) This dress goes perfectly with it.

15
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Wouldn’t you rather eat outside on the patio than inside the restaurant?

(A) That’s a great idea.

(B) I'll have a chicken sandwich.

(C) Yes, it’s nice and warm inside.

16
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Do you know if Bob is going to submit a proposal for funding?

(A) Yes, it was a fun time.

(B) He hasn’t decided yet.

(C) No, he doesn’t want to get married.

17
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Let’s go to the concert in Central Park this weekend.

(A) An award-winning piano manufacturer.

(B) I parked down the street.

(C) That sounds like fun.

18
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

Why did you return the desk lamp?

(A) Put the book back on the desk.

(B) Because it was damaged.

(C) Turn left at the light.

19
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Your client called twice while you were out.

(A) Did he leave a message?

(B) Try it once to see if it works.

(C) No, there are three.

20
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Can I bring you a dessert menu?

(A) Sure, here you go.

(B) No thanks, just the check, please.

(C) They put a recipe with the cookbook.

21
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Aren’t the computers on sale this week?

(A) Yes, the digital sailing watch works.

(B) I believe so.

(C) This computer was a donation.

22
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Do you want to take a morning or evening flight?

(A) The evening boxing match sounds exciting.

(B) I prefer to fly at night.

(C) Yesterday morning was a perfect choice.

23
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

How many interviews have you conducted today?

(A) To review résumés.

(B) The view is great from here.

(C) Only two so far.

24
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

Where do you want to go on Saturday?

(A) I went there last weekend.

(B) Anywhere you want.

(C) Every Sunday afternoon.

25
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

The new brochure logo looks good, doesn’t it?

(A) At the new print shop.

(B) The logo designer is at her desk now.

(C) It’s not too bad.

26
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

Why don’t we try the new Chinese restaurant that just opened?

(A) I suppose we could.

(B) No, I speak Cantonese.

(C) I’ve never been to China.

27
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Why wasn’t Eva in the office yesterday?

(A) She’s still out of town.

(B) She forgot to turn it off.

(C) I offered, but she refused.

28
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

You’re going to hire someone to do the painting, aren’t you?

(A) It depends on the cost.

(B) Hang it a little higher.

(C) They already finished painting the highway.

29
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

The item I want to buy is out of stock.

(A) Just put it on the shelf.

(B) Would you like a rain check?

(C) Oh, I see you bought some stocks.

30
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Where is your office located?

(A) Third and Main Street.

(B) From nine to five.

(C) Under the desks.

31
Đáp án đúng:A
