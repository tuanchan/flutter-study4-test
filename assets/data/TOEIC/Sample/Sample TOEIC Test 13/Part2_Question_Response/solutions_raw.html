Đáp án/transcript: Part 2 Thoát

Sample TOEIC Test 13

  • Part 2

Hiện Transcript

When does the warranty on this computer expire?

(A) For two years.

(B) A standard protection plan.

(C) A year from now.

7
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

How long have you been working in the Beijing office?

(A) Because I speak Mandarin.

(B) About ten hours a day.

(C) I transferred six months ago.

8
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

Have many customers responded to our survey?

(A) A customer satisfaction questionnaire.

(B) Quite a few.

(C) No, not too many questions.

9
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Which hotel should we use to book a room for our overseas client?

(A) One that isn’t too far from our office.

(B) It’s the client’s book.

(C) The room has a view of the ocean.

10
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Weren’t you supposed to set the alarm last night?

(A) No, I’m busy tonight.

(B) Yes, it was alarming.

(C) I thought I did.

11
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

Where can I buy stamps around here?

(A) Actually, the store next door sells them.

(B) The mail carrier left early last week.

(C) There’s a mailbox on the corner.

12
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Should I set up the projector for the slideshow?

(A) Did you like the movie?

(B) That would be great.

(C) The project report is complete.

13
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Do you know where Mr. Peterson’s office is?

(A) Only the marketing director.

(B) It’s just down the hall in room C.

(C) No, he isn’t.

14
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

This new printer works better than our old one, doesn’t it?

(A) Yes, it’s much more efficient.

(B) No, the new one is faster.

(C) She needed a couple more.

15
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Where in Brazil is the new employee from?

(A) He didn’t say.

(B) He finished on Monday.

(C) It’s the soccer team.

16
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Don’t you think we should take an umbrella?

(A) Let’s buy some take-out coffee.

(B) The black one.

(C) Is it supposed to rain?

17
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

How much is the train to Central Station?

(A) Ask the ticket agent.

(B) At Platform D.

(C) It’s four stops from here.

18
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Who was responsible for organizing the reception?

(A) I will have so many responsibilities.

(B) Jordan arranged everything.

(C) I didn’t know she was the new receptionist.

19
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Should we paint the office blue or green?

(A) Either one sounds fine to me.

(B) t don’t mind doing the work.

(C) The office paint hasn’t dried yet.

20
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

We should get a vending machine for the lunchroom.

(A) I just had a snack.

(B) There is one already?

(C) Let’s mention it at the meeting.

21
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

You need to wear a hardhat in the restricted area.

(A) Where can I get one?

(B) The area is difficult to reach.

(C) No, stricter quality-checking.

22
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Should we rent a small hall or a large one?

(A) The rent has been paid.

(B) It depends on the number of guests.

(C) I wasn’t able to go.

23
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Why don’t we hire an interior designer to do the work?

(A) It’s a beautiful design.

(B) The hair designer spoke with us.

(C) How much will that cost?

24
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

What time do you expect Mr. Samson to arrive?

(A) No, I don’t have the time.

(B) It’s ten to four o’clock.

(C) Between six and seven.

25
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

Why is the project so far behind schedule?

(A) Shipping issues, I think.

(B) His schedule is on the bulletin board.

(C) It’s not too far from here.

26
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

It would be cheaper to travel to Europe, wouldn’t it?

(A) Their lines are busy.

(B) Low prices in Brazil.

(C) That would be more economical.

27
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

I’d like to book a hotel room and rental car with my flight.

(A) OK, I have a great package deal for you.

(B) Sorry, that flight is full.

(C) I always read on the plane, too.

28
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Are we going to mark down our summer stock?

(A) The summer dresses are down there.

(B) He can spot a good bargain.

(C) Maybe next week.

29
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

When does this document need to go out?

(A) They stayed in yesterday.

(B) There’s no rush.

(C) A prescription from a doctor.

30
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Could you make me a copy of that article?

(A) Sure, I'll do it right away.

(B) They saw it in a magazine.

(C) Sorry, he will write an article.

31
Đáp án đúng:A
