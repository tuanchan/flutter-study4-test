(C) I don’t work on Saturdays.

(A) I didn’t expect you.

(B) On the fourth floor.

(C) I believe it’s Monday.

Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

(A) I don’t know what to wear.

(B) At the boat dock.

(C) Just a while ago.

Hiện Transcript

(C) The assistant used to be a project manager.

Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

(B) I’d love to join you.

(C) As an executive assistant.

Đáp án đúng:A

(C) Riding a tricycle is fun.

12
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

(B) She didn’t say.

(C) I looked everywhere.

Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

(C) Will our budget allow it?

Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

(C) Sign in at the front desk.

Đáp án đúng:A

(C) My appointment date isn’t set yet.

16
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

(C) No, I don’t have a car.

Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

(B) I don’t have any change.

(C) That can’t be right.

18
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

(B) Yes, very safe.

(C) Sure, come into my office.

19
Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

(B) Maybe two or three minutes.

(C) Room rates differ.

20
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

(A) Because she’s moving.

(B) I think he did.

(C) I prefer to stay here.

21
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

I hope to see you at the grand opening of our new store.

(A) Don’t worry, I'll be there.

(B) From nine to six.

(C) Sorry, I didn’t see you.

22
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

(A) A high-rise building.

(B) I’Il let you know tomorrow.

(C) On the north side.

23
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Do you want to hold the meeting in Mr. Lee’s office or yours?

(A) Well, my office is bigger.

(B) She accepted my offer.

(C) A private meeting.

24
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Didn’t you get reimbursed for your last business trip?

(A) To visit our overseas office.

(B) No, I’m still waiting.

(C) Yes, I brought the receipt for my new purse.

25
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Why don’t you join us for the retreat on the beach?

(A) He treated her to lunch.

(B) That sounds fun.

(C) Click “join” first.

26
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Could you give me a hand with this box?

(A) Her hand lotion was in the box.

(B) Certainly, I’d be glad to.

(C) In the storage area.

27
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

(A) No one yet.

(B) Lock up when you leave.

(C) I used a keyboard for the note.

28
Đáp án đúng:A
