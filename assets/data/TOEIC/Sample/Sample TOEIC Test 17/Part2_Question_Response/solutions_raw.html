(A) I'll wait for you.

(B) In the warehouse.

(C) At least fifteen.

Hiện Transcript

Where is the best place to park near the stadium?

(A) Usually on weekdays.

(B) On Sixteenth Avenue.

(C) A soccer tournament.

8
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

These file cabinets are for the sales department, aren’t they?

(A) A computer file.

(B) No, they’re for marketing.

(C) Yes, we were.

Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

(A) I just visit in person.

(B) I'd be happy to.

(C) Yes, she’s an accountant.

Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

(B) I’ll wash them now.

(C) Sadly, I missed it.

Đáp án đúng:C

Đáp án đúng:B

(C) The renovations look fantastic.

Hiện Transcript

(B) It’s too early to tell.

(C) A few times a year.

Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

(B) We’re completely sold out.

(C) Not until next week.

Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

(B) The top one.

(C) I'll hold it for you.

16
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

(C) Near the entrance.

Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

(C) We’re going to an art exhibition.

18
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

(B) I appreciate that.

(C) No, I don’t have time.

Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

(C) I don’t see why not.

Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

(B) Can Paul handle that?

(C) I’m a full-time member.

Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Are these ingredients still fresh?

(A) On the countertop.

(B) The farmer’s market.

(C) Ask the head chef.

Đáp án đúng:C

Hiện Transcript

(B) That’s correct.

(C) Delivery is free.

23
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Did Ms. Holbert accept any of our suggestions?

(A) Yes, I agree.

(B) She’s still considering them.

(C) You have a point.

24
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

Who's making the speech before the achievement awards?

(B) It’s the founder of the company.

(C) Congratulations! You deserve it!

25
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

This thermostat controls the entire building’s temperature, doesn’t it?

(A) No, we couldn’t find it.

(B) No, just this floor.

(C) I’m warm enough.

26
Đáp án đúng:B

Hiện Transcript

(B) The total is two hundred euros.

(C) That’s more than enough.

27
Đáp án đúng:A

Hiện Transcript

Are you going to Sunday’s orchestra concert?

(A) She plays the violin.

(B) How was it?

(C) Only if 1 can get a ticket.

28
Đáp án đúng:C

(C) That might be rather expensive.

Đáp án đúng:C
